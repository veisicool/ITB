ABOUT US

I.T.B. Ltd was founded in 1971. The company deals in management consultation supervision and monitoring of projects in legal, engineering, industrial, and financial fields.

The company meets the highest quality standards and has the standard of approval from ISO 9002. The company has two branches in Israel and the main office is located in the Industrial Zone of Lev HaSharon.

The company employs 90 professionals in various fields such as engineering, finance, architecture, and law.

Our Services

The engineering branch of the company deals in various fields which are:

  • Consultation, coordination, monitoring and supervision of building.
  • Infrastructure work & projects all over the country.
  • Management, planning and setting up of complex infrastructure systems.
  • Management, establishment and supervision of transportational projects.
  • Management, establishment and supervision of national projects (The Urban train in the Tel Aviv area, Ayalon highway, the desalination installation in Ashkelon & others).
  • Management and supervision of new villages, public buildings, hostels, apartments & places of business that are being established.

International Projects

The Israel Airports Authority

Project: ILAN & ASAF RAMON AIRPORT - EILAT

Budget: 1.6 B NIS

Ilan and Assaf Ramon Airport is the new airport of the city of Eilat replacing the airports of Eilat and Ovda. Ramon Airport Eilat is located about 19 km north of the city of Eilat and is actually the southern air gate of the State of Israel. The airport serves domestic and international tourism to the city of Eilat and to the southern resorts of the country, the Arava, Mitzpe Ramon, and the Dead Sea. The Ilan and Assaf Ramon Airport is a unique national project due to being the first international and domestic airport that was designed and built from the ground up by the Israel Airports Authority and is a testament to the Authority's expertise in the field of aviation. The airport has a 3,600-square-meter runway, which allows activity for most types of aircraft to any destination.

I.T.B Role: Monitoring & Control
